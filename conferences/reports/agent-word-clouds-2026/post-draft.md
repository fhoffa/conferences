# What do they mean by “agents”?

I looked across the 2026 catalogs for Google Cloud Next, Snowflake Summit, and Databricks Data + AI Summit.

Instead of just counting how often they say “agent(s),” I looked at the surrounding vocabulary in the sessions that mention the term.

The result: they are not talking about the same thing.

- Google Cloud talks about agents like a **platform layer**
- Snowflake talks about agents like **governed enterprise workflows**
- Databricks talks about agents like **production systems**

Same buzzword. Different worldview.

## Questions to answer
- Is agenda data embedded in page JSON or fetched via API/XHR?
- Are sessions segmented by day, track, or event type?
- Are speakers/session details fetched from a separate endpoint?
- What stable identifiers exist for sessions and speakers?
- Are there client-side rendering constraints that affect scraping?

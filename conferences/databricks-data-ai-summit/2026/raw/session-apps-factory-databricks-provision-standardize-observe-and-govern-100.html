Session

Apps Factory on Databricks​: Provision, Standardize, Observe and Govern 100+ Apps​

Overview

ExperienceIn Person
TrackApplication Development
IndustryHealthcare & Life Sciences, Manufacturing, Consulting & Services
TechnologiesDatabricks Apps, Lakebase
Skill LevelAdvanced

Databricks Apps makes it easy to publish the first app. We address the compounding challenges organizations face when scaling to 10, 50, or 100+ Databricks Apps: inconsistent architecture, unclear access control, missing standards, ungoverned AI components and runaway costs. We will walk through a comprehensive methodology, including solutions and principles to provision, govern and observe dozens of applications at enterprise scale. The session includes reference architecture, frameworks, templates, a production readiness checklist, CI/CD setup, security & access control, unified logging and cost tracking. Attendees receive a comprehensive technical governance and adaptation playbook to effectively manage and scale app ecosystems.
